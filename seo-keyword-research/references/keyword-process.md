# SEO Keyword Research — Process & Output Format

## Keyword Research Process (Web Search)

### Step 1: Competitor Discovery

Search queries:
1. `[primary keyword]` — see who ranks #1-5
2. `[primary keyword] [location]` — local competitors
3. `best [service] [location]` — review/comparison sites

Per top-5 competitor: domain URL, title tag, meta description, primary keyword, content depth.

### Step 2: Competitor Analysis Framework

| Analysis Point   | What to Look For                                    |
|:-----------------|:----------------------------------------------------|
| Title Tags       | Keywords, format, length (50-60 characters)         |
| Meta Descriptions| CTAs, keywords, USPs                                |
| H1 Headlines     | Primary keyword placement                           |
| Content Structure| H2/H3 topics                                        |
| Content Depth    | Word count, completeness                            |
| Weaknesses       | Gaps to exploit                                     |

### Step 3: Keyword Discovery

Search queries by intent:
```
[primary keyword] + "how", "what", "why"           (informational)
[primary keyword] + "buy", "book", "price"          (transactional)
[primary keyword] + "comparison", "best", "review"  (commercial investigation)
[primary keyword] + [location variations]
```

| Type        | Description                       | Priority      |
|:------------|:----------------------------------|:--------------|
| Primary     | Main target, high competition     | Must rank     |
| Secondary   | Supporting, medium competition    | Should rank   |
| Long-tail   | Specific, low competition         | Quick wins    |
| Local       | Geographic variations             | Expansion     |
| Semantic    | Related concepts (LSI)            | Natural usage |
| Question    | FAQ opportunities                 | Content       |

### Step 4: Search Intent Classification

| Intent                   | Signal Words                    | Content Type          |
|:-------------------------|:--------------------------------|:----------------------|
| Transactional            | buy, order, book, price, cost   | Service pages         |
| Commercial Investigation | comparison, best, review, vs    | Comparison pages      |
| Informational            | how, what, why, guide, tips     | Blog, FAQ             |
| Navigational             | [brand], [company name]         | Homepage, about       |

### Step 5: SERP Feature Analysis

| SERP Feature        | Impact on Strategy                                     |
|:--------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------|
| Featured Snippet    | Structured content (lists, tables)                     |
| Local Pack (Map)    | Optimize Google Business Profile                       |
| People Also Ask     | FAQ content opportunities                              |
| Related Searches    | Long-tail ideas                                        |

### Step 6: Keyword Difficulty Assessment

| Factor                 | Low               | High                          |
|:-----------------------|:------------------|:------------------------------|
| Competition            | Small local sites | Major brands/portals          |
| Content Quality        | Thin/outdated     | Comprehensive guides          |
| Backlinks              | Few               | Many quality backlinks        |
| Search Volume          | < 500/month       | > 5,000/month                 |

Rating: Easy (local competitors) → Medium (regional) → Hard (national) → Very Hard (portals).

### Step 7: Per-Page Keyword Mapping

Map keywords to pages from your sitemap:

| Page       | Primary Keyword | Secondary Keywords | Long-tail Keywords |
|:-----------|:----------------|:-------------------|:-------------------|
| Homepage   | [main keyword]  | [2-3 supporting]   | [2-3 specific]     |
| Services   | [service keyword]| [variations]      | [specific services]|
| About      | [brand + location]| [trust keywords]  | [differentiators]  |
| Contact    | [contact + location]| [booking keywords]| [specific CTAs]  |

Rules: one primary keyword per page, different intents on different pages.

### Step 8: Content Gap Analysis

1. Topics that competitors don't cover
2. Questions they don't answer (People Also Ask)
3. Local pages they're missing
4. Thin content you can expand on
5. Outdated competitor content
