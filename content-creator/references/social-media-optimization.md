# Social Media Optimization Guide

## Platform-specific best practices

### LinkedIn
**Audience**: B2B professionals, decision-makers, thought leaders
**Best times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM and 5-6 PM
**Optimal length**: 1,300-2,000 characters for posts

#### Content formats
- **Text posts**: 1,300 characters optimal, use white space
- **Articles**: 1,900-2,000 words, at least 5 images
- **Videos**: 30 seconds - 10 minutes, native upload preferred
- **Documents**: PDF carousels, 10-15 slides
- **Polls**: Max 4 options, 1-2 weeks duration

#### Optimization tips
- First 2 lines are crucial (shown in preview)
- Use emojis sparingly as visual breaks
- Add 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Tag people and companies where relevant
- Native video gets 5x more engagement
- Post consistently (3-5x per week optimal)

#### Algorithm factors
- Read time (time spent reading)
- Comments weigh more than likes
- Early engagement (first hour) is crucial
- Creator mode increases reach
- Replying to comments boosts visibility

### Twitter/X
**Audience**: News enthusiasts, tech fans, real-time conversations
**Best times**: Weekdays 9-10 AM and 7-9 PM
**Optimal length**: 100-250 characters

#### Content formats
- **Single tweets**: 250 characters, 1-2 hashtags
- **Threads**: 5-15 tweets, numbered format
- **Images**: 16:9 ratio, max 4 per tweet
- **Videos**: Max 2:20, square or landscape
- **Polls**: 2-4 options, 5 minutes - 7 days

#### Optimization tips
- Most important information first
- Use threads for complex topics
- Add visuals (2-3x more engagement)
- Quote tweet > plain retweet
- Schedule threads for consistency
- Engage genuinely with replies

### Instagram
**Audience**: Visual-oriented, millennials and Gen Z, lifestyle
**Best times**: Weekdays 11 AM - 1 PM and 7-9 PM
**Optimal length**: 138-150 characters shown in preview

#### Content formats
- **Feed posts**: Square (1:1) or portrait (4:5)
- **Stories**: Max 15 seconds, portrait (9:16)
- **Reels**: 15-90 seconds, portrait (9:16)
- **Carousels**: 2-10 images/videos
- **Video**: 1-60 minutes

#### Optimization tips
- First sentence is crucial (caption preview)
- Use up to 30 hashtags (5-10 in caption, rest in comment)
- Carousel posts get the most engagement
- Stories with polls/questions increase views
- Reels get maximum organic reach
- Post consistently (1-2 feed posts per day)

### Facebook
**Audience**: Broad demographics, community-focused, local businesses
**Best times**: Wednesday-Friday, 11 AM - 2 PM
**Optimal length**: 50-80 characters for posts

#### Content formats
- **Text posts**: 50-80 characters optimal
- **Images**: 1200x630px for links
- **Videos**: 1-3 minutes, square format
- **Stories**: Same as Instagram
- **Live videos**: Minimum 10 minutes

#### Optimization tips
- Native video gets priority
- Ask questions to stimulate comments
- Community content over promotional
- Share user-generated content
- Go live for maximum reach

## Repurposing strategy

One blog post yields:
- 1 LinkedIn post (core message + insight)
- 1 newsletter block (summary + link)
- 1-2 Instagram/Facebook posts (visual quote or tip)
- 1 Twitter/X thread (key takeaways as numbered list)

## Hashtag strategy

- **Research**: Check hashtag volume and relevance before using
- **Mix**: Combine broad (500K+), medium (10K-500K), and niche (< 10K) hashtags
- **Branded**: Create one unique branded hashtag
- **Limit**: LinkedIn 3-5, Instagram 5-15, Twitter 1-2
- **Placement**: LinkedIn/Twitter in post, Instagram split between caption and first comment
