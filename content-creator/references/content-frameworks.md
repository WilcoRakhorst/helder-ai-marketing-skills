# Content Templates and Frameworks

## Content types and templates

### 1. Blog post templates

#### How-to template
```markdown
# How to [achieve desired result] in [timeframe]

## Introduction
- Hook: Question or surprising fact
- Problem statement
- What the reader will learn
- Why it's relevant now

## What you need
- Tool/resource 1
- Tool/resource 2
- Estimated time

## Step 1: [Action]
- Clear instruction
- Why this step matters
- Common mistakes
- Visual example

## Step 2: [Action]
[Repeat structure]

## Step 3: [Action]
[Repeat structure]

## Troubleshooting common issues
### Issue 1: [Description]
**Solution**: [Fix]

### Issue 2: [Description]
**Solution**: [Fix]

## Results you can expect
- Immediate outcomes
- Long-term benefits
- Success criteria

## Next steps
- Advanced techniques
- Related guides
- CTA

## Conclusion
- Summary of key points
- Emphasize value
- Final encouragement
```

#### Listicle template
```markdown
# [Number] [adjective] ways to [achieve goal] in [year]

## Introduction
- Context/trend driving this topic
- Promise of what the reader gets
- Credibility statement

## 1. [First item — most important]
**Why it matters**: [Brief explanation]
**How to implement**: [2-3 concrete steps]
**Tip**: [Expert insight]
**Example**: [Practical application]

## 2. [Second item]
[Repeat structure]

[Continue for all items]

## Bonus tip: [Extra value]
[Something especially valuable]

## Bringing it all together
- How items complement each other
- Priority order
- Expected timeline

## Your action plan
1. Start with [simplest item]
2. Continue with [next steps]
3. Measure [criteria]

## Conclusion and CTA
```

#### Case study template
```markdown
# How [company] achieved [result] with [solution]

## Summary
- Company overview
- Challenge
- Chosen solution
- Key results (3 metrics)

## The challenge
### Background
- Industry context
- Company situation
- Previous attempts

### Specific pain points
- Pain point 1
- Pain point 2
- Pain point 3

## The solution
### Strategy development
- Assessment process
- Strategic approach
- Why this solution

### Implementation
- Phase 1: [Timeline and actions]
- Phase 2: [Timeline and actions]
- Phase 3: [Timeline and actions]

## The results
### Quantitative outcomes
- Metric 1: X% increase
- Metric 2: $Y saved
- Metric 3: Z improvement

### Qualitative benefits
- Team feedback
- Customer response
- Market position

## Key takeaways
1. Lesson learned
2. Best practice discovered
3. Unexpected benefit

## How to achieve similar results
- Prerequisites
- Implementation roadmap
- Success factors

## CTA: Start your success story
```

#### Thought leadership template
```markdown
# [Provocative statement about the future of the industry]

## The current situation
- Industry overview
- Common assumptions
- Why the status quo falls short

## The emerging trend
### What's changing
- Driver 1: [Technology/market/behavior]
- Driver 2: [Technology/market/behavior]
- Driver 3: [Technology/market/behavior]

### Evidence and examples
- Data point 1
- Case example
- Expert validation

## Implications for [industry]
### Short term (6-12 months)
- Immediate adjustments needed
- Quick wins available
- Risks of inaction

### Long term (2-5 years)
- Fundamental shifts
- New opportunities
- Competitive landscape

## Strategic recommendations
### For leaders
- Strategic priorities
- Investment areas
- Organizational changes

### For practitioners
- Skill development
- Process adaptation
- Tool adoption

## The way forward
- Call to action for the industry
- Your organization's role
- Next steps for readers

## Join the conversation
- Provocative question
- Invitation to share perspectives
- CTA for deeper engagement
```
